Equipment

Equipment Stats

    Players can use equipment equal or below their player level to enchance their stats

    Equipment can have a combination of 12 different stat improvements that kick in when the equipment is being used:

  • : Increases your total max health
  • : Increases your health gained over time
  • : Increases your resistance to damage
  • : Increases your basic attack's damage
  • : Increases the %chance for your hit to deal more damage according to your critical strike damage%
  • : Increases How much damage a weapon deals when it critically strikes
  • : Increases your total max mana
  • : Increases your mana gained over time
  • : Increases your total max stamina
  • : Increases your stamina gained over time
  • :Improves your running speed
  • : Increases the %chance for a basic attack to additionally strike for no stamina cost

Apart from and , all other stats are considered sub-stats and get randomly rolled on equipment

For every 10 equipment levels, sub-stats will be rolled an additional time:
e.g. (equipment level 10-19 has 1 sub-stat, level 20-29 has 2 sub-stats, etc.)

* the same sub-stat can be rolled more than once on a weapon:
e.g. (a level 100 dagger could have nothing but a massive amount of crit Chance% by rolling it 10 times)

Combat styles

Certain weapons are much more effective when wielded properly in its own combat style.
Using a greatsword in each hand never worked for anyone...

  • Rogue

    A rogue carries two daggers, one in each hand. In doing so, they can use the attributes of both weapons at the same time.

    Effect: 1. Weapon damage = 75% of both daggers' damage
    2. the sub-stats of both weapons are applied

  • Knight

    A knight carries both a longsword and a shield. In doing so, they can focus on both offense and defense simultaneously.

    Effect: Longsword deals 25% more damage | Shield Defense increases by 10%

  • Slayer

    A slayer wields a singular greatsword with both hands (off-hand empty). As such, the mighty weapon is easier to swing and can deal more devastating blows

    Effect: Greatsword deals 50% more damage
