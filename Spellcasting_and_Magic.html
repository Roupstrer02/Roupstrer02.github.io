Spellcasting and Magic

Your Spellbook

All players have 4 spells that they can cast:

  • (RIGHT-CLICK)
    Generally a spell usable in most situaltions
  • (RIGHT-CLICK MID-AIR)
    A spell usually focused on mobility
  • (RIGHT-CLICK + SPRINT)
    Usually a spell that is useful while moving
  • (RIGHT-CLICK + CROUCH)
    A grand spell of high cost and great power.

  • Casting Priority order

    In the case where the casting requirements for one or more spells are met, the spell you cast is determined by a simple priority order:

    1. 1. Ultimate Spell
    2. 2. Aerial Spell
    3. 3. Dash Spell
    4. 4. Standard Spell

Spell Selection Menu

Find your way here by using the /profile menu as described here: Everything about You

Spells are split into 4 schools of magic:

  • Wild offense and wave clear.
  • Ignoring and/or breaking enemies' defense.
  • Heal, protect, and buff allies.
  • Everyday magic. no combat utility, focuses on convenience.

    • Each column in the menu displays spells from each of the four schools of magic,
    and each row in the menu shows all the spells for a given spell slots.

    Therefore, players will pick one spell from each row to create their spellbook

Wands

Wands are the one and only universal tool witches and wizards use to cast any and all spells.

The effectiveness of a wand is divided into four attributes:

  • :
    The extra damage dealt by spells cast by the wand
  • :
    The additional potency of healing and protection spells cast by the wand
  • :
    The additional effectiveness of spells cast by the wand
    (extra range, area of effect, duration, etc.)
  • :
    The reduced mana cost of spells cast with the wand

Wand Crafting

Cores

  • Every wand is built around one of four cores of varying strength:
  • The core determines the wand's appearance and multiplies the effects of the amplifiers used to craft it.

Amplifiers

  • A wand can be crafted with up to 8 amplifiers from the following list:
  • Amplifiers add affinity to wands they are added to. Rarer materials offer more total affinity and can make more powerful wands.
    * There is no such thing as "one wand to rule them all". There is no objectively "best" amplifier

How to Craft

  1. 1. Place your chosen core in the center of your hotbar (5th slot)
  2. 2. add the amplifiers you wish to use on the other 8 slots of your hotbar
    (the order doesn't matter)
  3. 3. Right-Click the enchantment table

If you were successful, the appearance of your core should have changed into your new wand, otherwise, you will see an error in the chat for what went wrong
