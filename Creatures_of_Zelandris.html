Creatures of Zelandris

Weather & Biomes

At the start of each day, every is assigned a condition

All regular mobs spawn with a level determined by two factors: the it spawns in, and the in that biome

There are 13 weather conditions, each with their own difficulty rating (ranging from 0-12). No two biomes groups ever have the same weather.

The biome will also factor in to the mob's level. Some biomes make mobs easier, while others make them much harder

Experience

All regular creatures killed always drop EXP and equipment according to their level.

Awarded_EXP = ((2 ^ ((mobLevel+1) / 5) - 2 ^ (mobLevel / 5)) * 5)

EXP_Needed_For_LevelUp = (2^((playerLevel+1)/5) - 2^(playerLevel)/5) * 50
