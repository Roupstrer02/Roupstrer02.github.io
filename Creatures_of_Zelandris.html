Creatures of Zelandris

Mob Loot

Experience

All regular creatures killed always drop EXP and equipment according to their level.

Equipment

A single piece of equipment is always dropped on a mob kill and is attributed to the player who dealt the killing blow on the mob.
Equipment comes in 5 rarites:
Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary
