Runes

Any elite creature can drop runes. Using and anvil, runes can be added to equipment to further improve some of that equipment's stats, changing the item's name in the process.

Rune Varieties:

Damage: +15%
Crit Damage: +20%

Crit Chance: +8%
Multi Hit: +12%

Max Stamina: +1
Stamina Regen: +1/s

Max Health: +10%
Health Regen: +25%
Defense: +5%

Max Mana: +20%
Mana Regen: +1/s

Movement Speed: +100%

Combination Runes

Runes can either be used on their own, or combined with another rune to create a greater rune, adding the effects of both runes together:

Damage: +15%
Crit Damage: +20%

Max Mana: +20%
Mana Regen: +1/s

Damage: +15%
Crit Damage: +20%
Max Mana: +20%
Mana Regen: +1/s

Using Runes

Each piece of equipment can only slot 1 rune, applying a different rune onto a piece with an existing rune, replaces the old rune with the new one
