Welcome to Zelandris

Lore of the World

The world waged war over sources of primal magic: Creation, Order and Carnage. A group of three adventurers crossing the borders of nations set out to find the three primordial sources before the world’s superpowers could use it to bring ruin on one another. The adventurers gathered the sources from their resting places across the world: Creation, from the forests of Iliasing, Carnage, from the blazing deserts near Tu’Hra, and Order from the crystal cave of the eye of the storm. To keep the sources safe, each adventurer chose to fuse themselves with one of the sources, and disappear. Unbeknownst to them, the gathering of the three sources in one place caused the world to nearly crack open. This event was later named The Shattering.
