Everything about You

Your Skills

Your character's abilities are split into 7 skills:

  • : Represents your health and general constitution
  • : Represents your ability to persevere through damage
  • : Represents how hard you hit enemies, swing a sword, or fire a bow
  • : Represents your agility, speed, and affinity for quick strikes
  • : Represents your affinity for connecting with the magic in the world
  • : Represents the potency of the magic you possess
  • : Represents your ability to create things, let it be cooking, crafting or smithing

  • Defeating the monsters roaming around Zelandris will grant you experience points which contribute towards leveling up your character.
    Each level you gain grants you an additional skill point to add to 1 of your 7 skills.
